The Art of Assembly Language Programming contains many sample programs. The PDF and Postscript versions of the text also include lab manuals that make use of several pieces of Windows-based software. In addition to the software written specifically for Art of Assembly, the text also makes extensive use of the "UCR Standard Library for 80x86 Assembly Language Programmers." If you intend to use software from the text, or work along with the examples in the text, or do any of the assignments in the text, you will need to download the software associated with "The Art of Assembly Language Programming."
